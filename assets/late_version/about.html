Project Concept

OCAD GD Project

Created by Sihyun Park

Congruence vs. Similarity

In this project, completeness is defined as Congruence and incompleteness as Similarity. Rather than viewing incompleteness as a failure, I frame it as a valuable result of the continuous act of trying, a journey rather than a final status. The open-ended nature of these games reflects this philosophy, allowing players to perpetually strive for perfection in a process that has no fixed destination. By refining our senses through practice to approach machine-level precision, we explore the transition from similarity toward congruence, highlighting the beautiful growth found within our inherent human potential.

Game Modes

  • Speedrun: Test your instant HSL slider reflexes.
  • Memory Match: Reproduce a color purely from short-term memory.
  • Paint Drop: Experience analog RGB mixing.
  • Color Grid: Find the exact match among slightly altered tiles.
  • Odd One Out: Identify the single microscopic color difference.
